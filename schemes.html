📜 Government Schemes for Women

👧 Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

A savings scheme for girl children under age 10, offering high interest and tax benefits.

  • Interest rate: ~8%
  • Min deposit: ₹250/year
  • Max: ₹1.5 lakh/year
  • Apply at post office or bank

🏦 Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

Zero-balance savings account with debit card, insurance, and financial services access.

  • No minimum balance
  • Free RuPay debit card
  • ₹2 lakh accidental insurance
  • Open at any bank

🧵 Stand-Up India Scheme

Loans from ₹110 lakh to ₹11 crore for women entrepreneurs to start a new business.

  • Only for greenfield (new) enterprises
  • Available for women, SC/ST
  • Apply via standupmitra.in

💡 Ujjwala Yojana

Provides free LPG gas connections to women from below-poverty-line (BPL) households.

  • Upto ₹1600 subsidy
  • Gas stove & refill assistance
  • Apply via LPG distributor / CSC

📱 Mahila E-Haat

An online platform by the government to support women entrepreneurs and SHGs to showcase products and sell online.

  • Completely free platform
  • No middleman — sell directly
  • Visit mahilaehaat-rmk.gov.in

💰 Mudra Yojana for Women

Provides micro-loans up to ₹110 lakh to women for small businesses or self-employment.

  • Three categories: Shishu, Kishore, Tarun
  • No collateral needed
  • Apply at any bank or MFI

🛍️ Working Women Hostel Scheme

Government-funded hostels for working women and childcare for their children.

  • Safe, affordable housing
  • Preference for single mothers, widows
  • Check availability here

🛡️ One Stop Centres (Sakhi Centres)

These centers offer support for women facing violence — legal aid, shelter, police, counseling in one place.

  • Available in most districts
  • Free legal, medical and psychological support
  • More info here

📲 How to Apply for These Schemes

  • Visit the nearest post office, CSC, or bank depending on the scheme
  • Ask for the scheme application form
  • Fill and submit with required documents (Aadhaar, income proof, etc.)
  • Track status online or via SMS
