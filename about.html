🌟 What is SakhiPay?

SakhiPay is more than just a website — it’s your friendly digital *Sakhi* (companion) that makes money matters simple, safe, and stress-free 💖

💡 Why SakhiPay?

  • 🌐 Designed especially for women across India — from metros to villages
  • 🗣️ Voice-enabled & supports native languages like Hindi & Tamil
  • 📱 Easy-to-use on mobile phones, even for first-time users
  • 🤝 Built to remove fear, build confidence, and promote financial freedom

🚀 What Can You Do with SakhiPay?

  • 💬 Talk to an AI-powered chatbot to learn about UPI, banks, and digital safety
  • 📊 Use budgeting tools & calculators for smart savings
  • 📜 Explore government schemes like PMJDY or Sukanya Samriddhi
  • 🎥 Watch easy videos in your language — no reading needed!
  • 📍 Find nearby banks, helplines, or safety centers with just a tap

🎯 Our Mission

Many women feel lost when it comes to digital finance — due to fear, lack of support, or language gaps. SakhiPay is our way of saying: "You’re not alone. We’re here to help you grow, earn, and own your money journey."

"You don’t need to know English or be rich to control your money. You just need the right support — and that’s what SakhiPay is for." 💪👩‍💻💜
