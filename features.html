🌟 Key Features of SakhiPay

💬 1. Talk to Sakhi – AI Chatbot
Ask questions about banking, UPI, safety tips, government schemes.
Works with voice or text.
Gives answers in English, Hindi, or Tamil.
Easy-to-use for first-time users.

🧮 2. Budget Calculator
Simple tool to manage monthly expenses.
Includes EMI calculator and savings planner.
Helps women plan family budgets easily.

🛡️ 3. Help & Safety
📞 Emergency helpline numbers (1091, 1930, 112, etc.)
🧭 Share live location with trusted contact.
🗺️ Map showing nearby police stations, hospitals (Coming soon)
💡 Real-life scam alerts & cybercrime complaint guidance.

📜 4. Women Schemes
List of popular govt schemes for women: PMJDY, MUDRA, Sukanya Samriddhi, Skill India, etc.
Each scheme includes:
Purpose, Benefits, Eligibility, How to apply.

📘 5. Learn Basics
Step-by-step guides on:
How to use UPI
How to open a bank account
How to save money
Uses simple language with visuals/audio.

📝 6. Daily Tips & Quiz
One short tip every day for safety or finance.
Fun quiz to test knowledge (scam detection, UPI usage, etc.)

🎥 7. Watch & Learn
Short videos in Hindi, Tamil, and English.
Explainers on digital banking, frauds, and schemes.
Audio versions for non-literate users.

📂 8. Documents Needed
Clear checklist of what documents are required for:
Bank account, Loans, Government schemes, UPI activation.
Helps reduce confusion.

📍 9. Find Nearby Bank/ATM
Uses location to suggest nearby:
Banks, ATMs, Help centers (Coming soon)

🌐 10. Multi-Language Support
App works in English, Hindi, and Tamil.
All text, tips, and chatbot can change based on selected language.
