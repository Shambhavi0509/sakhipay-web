📄 Documents Needed for Financial Services & Schemes

In this section, we help you understand what documents are usually needed for various financial tasks — like opening a bank account, applying for a loan, starting UPI, or getting benefits from government schemes. Keeping the right documents ready makes the process smoother and faster.

🏦 1. Documents to Open a Bank Account

  • Aadhaar Card (for identity and address proof)
  • PAN Card (or Form 60)
  • Passport-size Photographs
  • Address Proof (Voter ID, Electricity Bill, Ration Card)
  • Mobile Number (linked with Aadhaar)

📝 Note: Many banks offer Jan Dhan Accounts with zero balance and simplified KYC.

💸 2. Documents for Loan Application

  • Aadhaar Card & PAN Card
  • Bank Passbook/Statements (last 6 months)
  • Income Proof (Salary Slips, Income Certificate)
  • Business Proof (GST certificate/shop registration)
  • Passport-size Photographs

📝 Tip: Always check with the bank for scheme-specific requirements.

👧 3. Documents for Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

  • Birth Certificate of the Girl Child
  • Identity & Address Proof of Guardian
  • Initial Deposit Amount (Min ₹250)

📝 Can be opened at Post Offices or banks like SBI, PNB.

👩‍💼 4. Documents for Government Women Schemes

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Ration Card (if applicable)
  • Income & Caste Certificate
  • Passport-size Photographs

📝 Requirements may vary — always check official guidelines.

📱 5. Documents for UPI and Digital Payments

  • Smartphone with Internet
  • Bank Account linked with Mobile Number
  • ATM/Debit Card (for UPI PIN setup)
  • UPI App (BHIM, PhonePe, Paytm, GPay)

📝 Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or password. Use only official apps.

✅ Tips to Keep in Mind

  • Store soft copies on DigiLocker, Google Drive, or USB drives
  • Lost documents? File FIR and apply for duplicates
  • Verify schemes on official portals like mygov.in, nari.nic.in, or pmjdy.gov.in
