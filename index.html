SakhiPay

SakhiPay is an AI-powered, voice-enabled web application designed to help women learn and safely use digital payments — especially UPI — in their own language and at their own pace.

Learning Made Interactive

💬

Talk to Sakhi

Interactive chatbot with voice that answers questions on banking, UPI, schemes, and safety.

🧮

Budget Calculator

Simple tools for monthly budget, EMI, interest calculator, etc.

🛡️

Help & Safety

Emergency helplines, nearby help centers, safety tips for secure digital usage.

SakhiPay Tools & Guidance

📜 Women Schemes

Show PMJDY, Sukanya Samriddhi, etc. with eligibility & how-to-apply.

📘 Learn Basics

Step-by-step guides: how to use UPI, create a bank account, save money, etc.

🎯 Daily Tip / 📝 Quiz

Improve awareness with daily tips or small quizzes.

🎥 Watch & Learn

Short explainers in video/audio (in native languages) for non-literate users.

📍 Find Nearby Bank

Use location to find banks or ATMs near the user.

📂 Docs Needed

Checklist for loans, account opening, etc. — helps reduce confusion.
